Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Sacrada ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected when you visit sacrada.space and book a session, and how it is used.

1. Data we collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Booking data — when you book a session via Calendly, Calendly collects your name, email address and the date/time you select. We receive this information so we can prepare for your session.
  • Communications — if you contact us by email, we retain your message and contact details to respond to you.
  • Website analytics — this site may use anonymised, cookie-free analytics to understand how the site is used. No personal data is stored.

2. How we use your data

  • To confirm and prepare for your booked session.
  • To send you session reminders or follow-up information.
  • To respond to any enquiry you send us.

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-party services

Booking is handled by Calendly. When you book, you are subject to Calendly's own privacy policy (calendly.com/privacy). We recommend reviewing it before booking.

Payments are processed through the payment provider linked to your booking. We do not store payment card details.

4. Spiritual content disclaimer

Sacrada sessions involve breathwork, intuitive mediumship and somatic awareness. All content shared during sessions is intended for spiritual exploration and personal development only. Nothing in a session constitutes medical, psychological or legal advice.

5. Data retention

We retain booking and communication data for up to 12 months, after which it is securely deleted unless we are required by law to retain it longer.

7. Cookies
