The SUP Aventura Way

More than a yoga class.

SUP Aventura is Juli Kessler's curated experience brand rooted in Santa Teresa, Costa Rica. Whether you're seeking a private yoga session at sunrise, a heart-opening cacao ceremony, a transformative sound healing, or a full multi-day retreat woven with adventure — every experience is designed around you. Juli brings her deep love of this land, her spiritual practice, and her gift for connection to everything she creates.
